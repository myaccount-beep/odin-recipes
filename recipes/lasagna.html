Lasagna

Description

This is the world's best lasagna recipe, packed with rich meat sauce, layers of pasta, and a creamy blend of cheeses. It's the ultimate comfort food for family dinners!

Ingredients

  • Meat (Beef or Pork)
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ricotta and Mozzarella cheese
  • Tomato sauce

Steps

  1. Cook the meat sauce with tomato sauce and spices.
  2. Boil the lasagna noodles until tender.
  3. Layer the noodles, meat sauce, and cheeses in a baking dish.
  4. Bake in the oven until the cheese is melted and bubbling.
